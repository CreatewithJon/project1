## Purpose

This document defines the scope of Agentic Systems and establishes clear separation between it and Digital Wealth Transfer.

**The most important thing Claude must know:**
Agentic Systems is a **separate company**. It has no codebase in this repo. It has no routes on `digitalwealthtransfer.com`. It is not a DWT product or feature. It is a different business with its own team, projects, and IP.

---

## What Agentic Systems Is

**Agentic Systems** is an AI consulting, implementation, and education company. It builds AI systems for businesses, develops curriculum for workforce development, and operates community initiatives in the AI and digital assets space.

### Service Lines

**AI Implementation & Automation**
- AI lead capture and follow-up systems
- Automated appointment-setting workflows
- AI-powered chatbots and SMS bots
- CRM setup and lead tracking
- AI-optimized websites and funnels
- Custom AI automation for specific business processes

**Education & Workforce Development**
- AI curriculum development for professional and institutional programs
- The GH-600 / UNLV course proof-of-concept
- Workforce readiness programs
- Instructor-led workshops and lab-based learning

**Community**
- Crypto Mondays Las Vegas — chapter operated under Agentic Systems
